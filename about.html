.About

/Why Ai is Bias tries to understand the reason behind the bias in AI and its impact on society. This project consists of a print publication and this web archive: The print publication is a journey about AI: what AI means, how it is for society, how AI learns and how it fails. AI or Artificial Intelligence is a technology that shows how computer algorithms can learn, optimize and improve through the process of learning from data. Although learning is a difficult process and brings errors and faults, this could be caused by past data or humans. All of these influences bring bias to AI. Through the vision of Joler, Vladan & Matteo Pasquinelli, Kate Crawford & Trevor Paglen we can understand better AI and its bias. To complement the print publication is addressed this web archive that shows us 10 projects or works that explore bias in AI.
